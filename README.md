[Shopware Blog](https://magefan.com/shopware/extensions/blog) is an extension that facilitates blog management on Shopware and enables you to create multiple blog posts, categories, tags and authors. Create engaging content using user-friendly, SEO-optimized and easy-to-manage Blog Extensions for Shopware.

## Features
### Manage SEO-optimized Blog to Drive Traffic

Creating engaging content for your Shopware customers is one thing. Making that content rank in search for better traffic is the complete opposite. However, in our Shopware Blog Extension, we made sure to implement all of the most important SEO features. 

XML sitemap, canonical tags, meta data and meta tags robots enable you to improve your blog SEO and drive more free, organic traffic from Google. 


### Deliver Exceptional experience Across Multiple Devices
Responsiveness is a second important thing you have to pay attention to when setting up your blog. Stay assured your blog will look equally good on all devices. Desktop, tablet and mobile users will see responsive templates and attractive designs come together to deliver an exceptional experience. 

<p align="center">
  <img width="853" height="425" src="https://cm.magefan.com/catalog/responsive-shopware-blog.png">
</p>

### Create Unlimited Posts, Categories and Tags
Depending on your niche, you might have a lot of topics to cover. Since there is no limit to the number of blog posts, categories, tags or authors in our Shopware Blog Extension you can keep these topics organized and structured. 

<p align="center">
  <img width="853" height="425" src="https://cm.magefan.com/catalog/manage-blog-on-shopware.png">
</p>

### Manage Navigational Sidebar 
Make your blog not only engaging but easy to navigate so your customers can find the content they are interested in faster. Our Shopware Blog Extension is equipped with multiple widgets that contribute to that.

Enable blog search, categories tree, recent blog posts, archive, tags cloud and RSS feed. Make sure customers can find relevant content wherever they are on your blog.


<p align="center">
  <img width="853" height="425" src="https://cm.magefan.com/catalog/shopware-blog-sidebar_1.png">
</p>

### Enable Blog Comments
To get feedback and keep a dialogue with your customers you need blog comments. They help you to learn about the topics your readers like and what they think of your Shopware blog in general. This contributes greatly to your content strategy and helps to establish trust and authority.
